Relative Clauses

Who, That, Which — Identifying People and Things

GRAMMAR FOCUS
PEOPLE Do you know the man who lives next door?
PEOPLE Do you know the man that lives next door?
THINGS I lost the book that I borrowed from you.
THINGS I lost the book which I borrowed from you.

2Practice: Talking About People
1. Do you know the man 📷 fixed our roof?
2. She's the teacher 📷 taught me English.
3. He's the neighbor 📷 always feeds the cats.
4. They're the students 📷 won the science fair.
5. She's the doctor 📷 treated my father.
6. He's the man 📷 lost his wallet.
3Practice: Talking About Things
1. Where's the umbrella 📷 I left here?
2. I returned the sweater 📷 didn't fit.
3. This is the recipe 📷 won first prize.
4. I fixed the bike 📷 was broken.
5. That's the song 📷 played at our wedding.
6. She found the keys 📷 went missing.
4Let's Talk!
Work with a partner. Ask and answer questions using relative clauses.
  • Tell me about a person who inspires you.
  • Describe something that you bought recently and like.
  • Talk about a movie which made you cry or laugh.
  • Describe a place that changed the way you think.
