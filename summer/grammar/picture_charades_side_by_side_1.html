How to play
  1. Split into Team A and Team B.
  2. Click New Picture — only the describer should look at the screen (others look away or the describer reads it aloud as a clue-giver).
  3. Start the 60-second timer. The describer explains what's happening in the picture without saying the obvious key word (e.g. say "he's playing it for the recital" instead of "piano").
  4. Their team shouts out guesses until they get it, time runs out, or they skip.
  5. Click Reveal Answer to check the book's intended action, then award a point to whichever team guessed correctly.
  6. Click New Picture and pass the turn to the other team.
  7. Most points when the pictures run out (or after a set number of rounds) wins!
